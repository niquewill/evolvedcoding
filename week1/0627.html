    How To Make A Cheeseburger PoBoy


  1. Get Some Fresh Leidenheimer Bread
  2. Toast it in the oven for 3 minutes
  3. Put Two Slices of Cheese, some lettuce, and sliced tomatoes
  4. Then, Put to 1/3LB grass fed hamburger patties on the bread
  5. Spread with whatever you enjoy, I like creole mustard
  6. Now, cut in half and share with a stranger. Hmmm
